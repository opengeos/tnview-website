# Funding Opportunities

TennesseeView provides several funding opportunities for Tennessee students working on projects that utilize remote sensing and Earth observation. These opportunities include:

- TennesseeView Fellowships
- Student Award Competitions
- Undergraduate Research Opportunities
- Student Research Webinars

## TennesseeView Fellowships

The TennesseeView Fellowship Program aims to provide support, encouragement, and resources to students in Tennessee who are interested in pursuing a career in geospatial data science and remote sensing. These fellowships also aim to increase the awareness of geospatial technology in STEM education and workforce development.

**Announcement**

- [TennesseeView Fellowships 2026](https://tnview.org/TNView-Fellowships-2026.pdf)


**Eligibility**

- Must be a current undergraduate, graduate, or post-baccalaureate student at a college or university in the state of Tennessee.
- Open to students from any discipline.
- Must have good academic standing.
- Must be working on a project that utilizes remote sensing and Earth observation, with a requirement to present at the TennesseeView webinar.
- Must not have previously received the TennesseeView fellowship.

**Application Checklist**

- Full name
- Current undergraduate or graduate status
- Department and university
- Phone number and email address
- Name and email address of faculty advisor
- A cover letter of no more than 2 pages that clearly identifies the problem, its significance, the planned approach, and a stakeholder (e.g., a state agency, community organization, or school/university)
- Resume or CV

**Number of Awards**

- Graduate students: 3 fellowships available at \$500 each.
- Undergraduate students: 3 fellowships available at \$500 each.

**Application Deadline:** April 30, 2026

Please submit your application at [tiny.utk.edu/tnview-apply](https://tiny.utk.edu/tnview-apply).

## Student Award Competitions

The remote sensing competition awards at the [TNGIC Annual Conference](https://www.tngic.org/events) consist of one first-place award valued at \$300, two second-place awards valued at \$200 each, and three third-place awards valued at \$100 each.

## Undergraduate Research Opportunities

TennesseeView provides opportunities for undergraduate students to work on remote sensing projects. Undergraduate students who are interested in working on remote sensing projects should contact Dr. Qiusheng Wu at [tnview@utk.edu](mailto:tnview@utk.edu).

**Eligibility**

- Must be a current undergraduate student at the University of Tennessee, Knoxville
- Open to students from any discipline
- Must have good academic standing
- Must be interested in geospatial data science and remote sensing

Please contact Dr. Qiusheng Wu at [tnview@utk.edu](mailto:tnview@utk.edu).
